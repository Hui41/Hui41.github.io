Let ξ:V→M be a smooth bundle over Mm of finite rank.

Suppose W is another bundle. We define:\

direct sum connection:

∇X(σ⊕λ)=∇XNσ⊕∇XWλ

tensor product connection:

∇X(σ⊗λ)=(∇XVσ)⊗λ+σ⊗(∇XWλ)

the dual connection on V∗:

(∇Xθ)(σ)=X(θ(σ))−θ(∇Xσ),∀σ∈Γ(V)
